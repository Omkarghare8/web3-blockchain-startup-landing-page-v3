✦ FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about NexaChain, our technology, and how to get started.

What is NexaChain?
NexaChain is a next-generation blockchain platform designed for high-performance decentralized applications. We combine cutting-edge cryptography with innovative consensus mechanisms to deliver unparalleled speed, security, and scalability for Web3 applications.
How do I stake NXC tokens?
Staking is simple: connect your Web3 wallet, navigate to the Staking section, choose your preferred staking pool and duration, then confirm the transaction. You'll start earning rewards immediately, with APY rates up to 25% depending on your lock-up period.
What makes NexaChain different?
NexaChain features our proprietary Quantum-Proof consensus algorithm, achieving 100,000+ TPS with sub-second finality. Our unique architecture enables seamless cross-chain communication while maintaining enterprise-grade security and full EVM compatibility.
Is the platform audited?
Yes, NexaChain has undergone comprehensive security audits by leading firms including CertiK, Trail of Bits, and OpenZeppelin. All audit reports are publicly available on our GitHub repository for complete transparency.
How can I build on NexaChain?
Developers can get started with our comprehensive SDK and documentation. We support Solidity smart contracts, offer extensive APIs, and provide grants for promising projects. Join our Developer Discord for support and collaboration opportunities.
Where can I buy NXC tokens?
NXC tokens are available on major centralized exchanges including Binance, Coinbase, and Kraken, as well as decentralized exchanges like Uniswap and our native NexaSwap. Always verify contract addresses through our official channels.
